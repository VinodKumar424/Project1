ABOUT ME

Nick Boes

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA

I am a visual designer and animator originally from Northwest Ohio and based in Los Angeles
with over 10 years of experience in the industry. I am a passionate creative that leads by
example with a hands-on approach. I believe design is earned. An idea, a concept, a
masterpiece is only as powerful as the message it is able to carry. I work alongside my team
no matter the situation or limited time to teach. I seek to inspire, and build the best work
possible. I am a critical thinker and problem solver that takes a holistic approach to every
project, making sure every aspect of it gets produced at the highest quality.
